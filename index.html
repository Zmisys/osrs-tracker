Your companion for Old School RuneScape progression

Welcome to OSRS Tracker

OSRS Tracker is a specialized progression tracking tool designed for both regular and Ironman accounts. Whether you're a new player or returning to Gielinor, this tool helps you track your progress and achieve your goals efficiently.

Features

📊 Progress Tracking

Monitor your skill levels, quest completion, and achievement progress in real-time.

🎯 Goal Setting

Set and track your OSRS goals, from skill milestones to diary completions.

📚 Wiki Integration

Quick access to OSRS Wiki for items, quests, and game mechanics.

💡 Recommendations

Get personalized recommendations based on your account progression.

⚔️ Ironman Support

Specialized features and tracking for Ironman and Ultimate Ironman accounts.

🚀 Beginner Friendly

Perfect for new players with guides and tips for efficient progression.
